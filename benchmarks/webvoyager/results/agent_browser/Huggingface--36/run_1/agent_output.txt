## HuggingFace Pricing — Payment Plans Summary

HuggingFace offers **three subscription tiers** plus **usage-based pricing** for compute and storage.

### Subscription Plans

**1. PRO Account — $9/month**
*Boost your personal HF experience.* Advantages:
- 10× private storage & 2× public storage capacity
- 20× included inference credits
- 8× ZeroGPU quota + highest queue priority
- Spaces Dev Mode & ZeroGPU Spaces hosting
- Personal blog publishing
- Dataset Viewer for private datasets
- PRO badge to show support

**2. Team — $20/month per user** *(Most popular)*
*Instant setup for growing teams.* Advantages:
- SSO support (SAML & OIDC)
- Data location control via Storage Regions
- Audit Logs for action reviews
- Granular access control with Resource Groups
- Repository usage Analytics
- Advanced auth policies & visibility controls
- Centralized token control and approvals
- Dataset Viewer for private datasets + advanced Spaces compute
- All members get ZeroGPU & Inference Providers PRO benefits

**3. Enterprise — $50/month per user**
*Custom onboarding + enterprise features.* Advantages:
- **All Team benefits**, plus:
- Highest storage, bandwidth & API rate limits
- Automated user management with SCIM provisioning
- Advanced security and access controls
- Managed billing with annual commitments
- Legal and Compliance processes
- Dedicated support

### Usage-Based Pricing (pay-as-you-go)
- **Storage:** Volume-based, **$8–12/TB/month** (cheaper than AWS S3 $23 / Backblaze $15); egress & CDN included; volume discounts up to −33% at 500TB+.
- **Spaces Hardware:** On-demand GPU **starting at $0** (CPU Basic & ZeroGPU free; up to 8× A100 at $20/hr).
- **Inference Endpoints:** Dedicated, autoscaling inference **starting at $0.033/hour** (no cold starts).

(1) **PRO — $9/mo**: personal boost — 10× private/2× public storage, 20× inference credits, 8× ZeroGPU quota + priority, Spaces Dev Mode, blog publishing, private Dataset Viewer, PRO badge. (2) **Team — $20/mo per user** (most popular): for orgs — SSO (SAML/OIDC), Storage Regions, Audit Logs, Resource Groups, Analytics, advanced auth/token controls, plus PRO benefits for all members. (3) **Enterprise — $50/mo per user**: all Team features plus highest limits, SCIM provisioning, advanced security, managed/annual billing, compliance processes, and dedicated support. Additionally: Storage at $8–12/TB/mo (egress/CDN included), Spaces GPU hardware on-demand starting at $0 (free CPU/ZeroGPU tiers), and dedicated Inference Endpoints from $0.033/hour.
