**Brief summary of what ESPN+ is used for** (per ESPN's official page):
ESPN+ is ESPN's paid streaming service used to watch live sports and exclusive content. It provides access to **more than 32,000 live sports events annually** — top-tier soccer, PGA Tour golf, NHL, UFC, tennis, a selection of college sports, PLL, UFL and other leagues — plus a **robust library of exclusive studio shows, on-demand replays, and acclaimed ESPN original content**. In ESPN's new direct-to-consumer setup, this ESPN+ content is delivered through the **ESPN Select Plan** ($11.99/mo or $119.99/yr), and is also bundled into the higher-tier ESPN Unlimited Plan and Disney+/Hulu bundles. The enhanced ESPN app additionally integrates tools like game stats, betting info, fantasy sports, multiview, and a personalized "SportsCenter For You."
