The Apple Repair page (support.apple.com/repair) describes several ways to get an Apple product serviced:
- **Apple retail stores** — Apple-certified repairs using genuine parts
- **Apple Authorized Service Providers** — certified repairs covered by warranty/AppleCare
- **Self Service Repair** — repair your own device with genuine Apple parts, tools, and manuals
- Independent (non-authorized) repair providers

ANSWER: Two Apple Repair ways mentioned are (1) repair at an Apple retail store / Apple Authorized Service Provider, and (2) Self Service Repair (repair your own device using genuine Apple parts, tools, and manuals).
