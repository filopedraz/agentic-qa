**Ingredients:**
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 red bell pepper, chopped
- 4 oz Spanish chorizo, diced
- 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cubed
- 1 (12 oz) package Arborio rice
- 5 cups chicken broth
- ½ cup white wine
- 1 sprig fresh thyme
- 1 pinch saffron
- Salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 squid, cleaned and cut into pieces
- 2 tomatoes, seeded and chopped
- ½ cup frozen green peas
- 12 large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 lb mussels, cleaned and debearded
- ¼ cup chopped Italian flat-leaf parsley
- 8 lemon slices, for garnish

**Total time:** 1 hour 30 minutes (45 min prep + 45 min cook)

**Preparation overview:**
1. Sauté onion, garlic, and bell pepper in olive oil; add chorizo, chicken, and rice and cook briefly. Stir in 3½ cups broth, wine, thyme, and saffron; season, then simmer ~15 min.
2. Check the rice and add more broth as needed (up to 5 cups total), cooking until the rice is done.
3. Stir in squid, tomatoes, and peas; cook 2 min. Arrange shrimp and mussels on top, cover with foil, and let sit 3–5 min until cooked.
4. Remove foil, scatter parsley, and serve in the paella pan garnished with lemon wedges.
