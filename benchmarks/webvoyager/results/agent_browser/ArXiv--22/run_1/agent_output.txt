**How to subscribe to daily listing emails for a specific category:**

Send a **plain ASCII text** email (rich-text/UTF-8 is ignored) to the archive's address:

1. **Find the archive's email address** from the list of available archives (format: `<archive>@arxiv.org`).
2. **For archives divided into subject classes** (e.g. `math`, `physics`, `cs`, `q-bio`, `q-fin`) — email the archive and list the categories in the body. Example:
   ```
   To: physics@arxiv.org
   Subject: subscribe John Smith

   add Biophysics
   del Plasma Physics
   ```
   You may also use short codes, e.g. to subscribe to Risk Management (`q-fin.RM`):
   ```
   To: q-fin@arxiv.org
   Subject: subscribe John Smith

   add RM
   ```
3. **For archives not divided into subject classes** — just send:
   ```
   To: quant-ph@arxiv.org
   Subject: subscribe John Smith
   ```
4. **`astro-ph`, `cond-mat`, `nlin`** subsets are subscribed via the `physics` archive (e.g. `add Superconductivity`).

To cancel: email the archive with `Subject: cancel`.
