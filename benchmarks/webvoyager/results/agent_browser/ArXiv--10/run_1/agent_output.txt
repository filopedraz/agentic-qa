ANSWER: If your submission has not yet become publicly available (not yet announced), you can delete or delay it by going to your user page and selecting either the Delete icon (removes it from the system entirely) or the Unsubmit icon (returns it to incomplete status, allowing modification and later resubmission). If you have difficulty, contact arXiv administrators immediately, including the submission identifier, an explanation of the problem, and the reason for withdrawal.
